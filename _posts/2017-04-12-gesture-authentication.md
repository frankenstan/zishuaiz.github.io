$$T[N] = \{t_1, t_2,...,t_n, ...,tN \}$$

$$R[M] = \{r_1, r_2,...,r_m, ...,r_M \}$$

$$(t_n, r_m)$$

$$D(t_n, r_m) = d(t_n, r_m)+min(D(t_{n-1}, r_m),D(t_{n-1}, r_{m-1}), D(t_{n-2})).$$
$$d(t_n, r_m) = \sqrt{(t_n-r_m)^2}, 1 \le n \le N, 1 \le m \le M.$$
